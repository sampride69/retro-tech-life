Who TwinTrace is built for

Manufacturing SMEs

Companies with energy-intensive machinery, multiple production lines, or dispersed data across meters, spreadsheets, and ERPs. TwinTrace helps organize, measure, document, and prepare internal reports or responses to client and bank sustainability requests.

Suppliers to large groups

Organizations that receive ESG data requests from larger clients. The European VSME standard (adopted by the Commission in 2025) is reducing the administrative burden — TwinTrace helps you respond with structured, reliable data.

Audit, finance & incentive readiness

Companies that need traceable data, historical records, organized documentation, and presentable indicators — for audits, bank assessments, or public incentive programs.

Consultants & professional teams

Sustainability consultants, small teams, and professional operators who want a structured internal software base to support their client workflows and reporting deliverables.
