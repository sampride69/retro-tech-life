Secure Payments

All our transactions are encrypted and processed securely.

How We Process Payments

At Retro Tech Life, your security and privacy are our top priorities. That is why we have partnered with Gumroad, a trusted, industry-leading platform for digital product distribution and payment processing.

When you decide to purchase one of our products, systems, or kits, you will be redirected to our secure Gumroad checkout page. This ensures that your payment information is handled with the highest level of security.

Accepted Payment Methods

Through our secure checkout, we accept a wide variety of payment methods worldwide:

  • All major Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, Diner's Club)
  • PayPal (available for most countries and currencies)
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay (on supported devices)

Instant Access

Because our products are entirely digital, there is no waiting for shipping. As soon as your payment is successfully processed, you will receive an email containing a secure link to access, download, or duplicate your digital products immediately.

For any payment issues, duplicate charges, or to request an invoice, please contact us at mail.tetro.tech.life@gmail.com.
